Welcome to SelfLearner platform

What is SelfLearner?

SeflLearner is a website dedicated to people who are enjoying a process of learning a new language and are in need of a tool to create own flashcards

It not only allows to create your own database of resources but also lets you share them with your friends or students

Is SelfLearner a website for students only?

Teacher mode allows creating and sharing language courses. Within a course you can share your resources or post messages to your students
